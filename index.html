Scrim Commands Give your custom hosts the right tools to scrims in your server through Maven's commands.
!add solo [code] Sends your Solo Scrim announcement
!add duo [code] Sends your Duo Scrim announcement
!add trio [code] Sends your Trio Scrim announcement
!add squad [code] Sends your Squad Scrim announcement
!solo announce [message] Sends Solo Scrim information
!duo announce [message] Sends Duo Scrim information
!trio announce [message] Sends Trio Scrim information
!squad announce [message] Sends Squad Scrim information
!add msolo [code] Sends your Solo Money Scrim announcement
!add mduo [code] Sends your Duo Money Scrim announcement
!add mtrio [code] Sends your Trio Money Scrim announcement
!add msquad [code] Sends your Squad Money Scrim announcement
